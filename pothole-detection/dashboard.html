Your Safety is Our Priority

PotholeWatch utilizes state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence to automatically eliminate lethal road hazards across Kerala.

🧠

AI-Powered Vision

Our YOLO machine learning engine actively scans live camera feeds to automatically isolate dangerous road fractures, instantly calculating surface area and severity metrics.

🛰️

Accurate Telemetry

When a fracture is found, our system automatically tags the geographic location and uses reverse-geocoding to seamlessly route the report to either the NHAI or local Kerala PWD.

🚨

Live Driver Alerts

Acting as a smart dashcam, the app rapidly delivers auditory voice warnings to the driver regarding an upcoming pothole, preventing catastrophic vehicular accidents.
